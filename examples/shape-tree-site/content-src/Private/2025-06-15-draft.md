Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet. This file lives in `Private/`, a directory listed in
`AUTH_PRIVATE_DIRECTORIES`, so it is excluded from the index and navigation and

## Why it is hidden

- Posts are marked private when their directory matches `AUTH_PRIVATE_DIRECTORIES`.
- `ContentStore.publishedPosts` filters them out of the nav and index.
- The `/wasm/posts/:slug` route returns 404 for anonymous visitors instead of leaking the content.
